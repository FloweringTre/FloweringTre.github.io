Text Styling

The H1 and H2 headers are always present in the header image at the top of the page. The sign off at the bottom of the page is always in the H3 heading.

H1 - Main Page Headings

  • Font-wight: 400 (regular)
  • Font-size: 60px
  • Font-family: Itim
  • Color options: #FFFFFF
  • Alignment: Center

H2 - Main Page Subheadings

  • Font-wight: 400 (regular)
  • Font-size: 30px
  • Font-family: Calibri
  • Color options: #FFFFFF
  • Alignment: Center

H3 - In Article Headings

  • Font-wight: 400 (regular)
  • Font-size: 30px
  • Font-family: Itim
  • Color options: #81b2e3 or #c2e892
  • Alignment: center or left with indent

P - Paragraph Text

  • Font-wight: 400 (regular)
  • Font-size: 16px
  • Font-family: Source Code Pro
  • Color options: #FFFFFF
  • Alignment: Left
